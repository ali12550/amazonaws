What goes into CREATING your Product

PRE-PRESS

Design: Creating the artwork or document that will be printed.

  • File Preparation: Ensuring the digital file is in the correct format, color mode, and resolution for printing.
  • Proofing: Creating a test print to check for errors and make any necessary adjustments before the final print.
  • Plate Making: Creating printing plates or other image carriers based on the design

PRESS

Printing: This is the actual printing process where the design is transferred onto the chosen material.

  • Setting up the Printer: Loading the correct paper or substrate, setting up the ink or toner, and configuring the printing machine.
  • Running the Job: The printer executes the print job, transferring the design onto the material.

POST-PRESS

Finishing: Preparing the printed product for its intended use.

  • Cutting and Trimming: Ensuring the final product is the correct size and shape.
  • Binding, Coating and Lamination: For items like books or magazines, this step involves binding the pages together OR adding a protective layer.
  • Packaging: Shipping or Delivery.
